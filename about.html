About the Club:

Hello Flames Fans, Welcome to the Calgary Flames Club! Do you enjoy the flames? Want to find like-minded people like you? Want a greater sense of community? Don't look any farther as this club is all you could ask for as a fan. We do many things like get together to watch games, activities, prize pools, hockey drafts and more. We are actively looking for people who want to be part of our community and further elevate our experiences as a fan. If this sounds like you please use our contact page so we can get to know you better!
